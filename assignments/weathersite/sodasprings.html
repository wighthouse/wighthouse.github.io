Bank Robbery in Soda Springs

KIFI
Soda Springs Police are investigating the robbery of Ireland Bank. The robber entered the bank at 98 West Second Street shortly after noon Monday. He showed no weapons but handed the teller a note demanding money. Upon leaving the bank, he was seen getting into a small, white, two-door car. He was described as being a white male in his 20's, wearing a dark-colored jacket, a fake moustache, aviator glasses and what appeared to be a long, dark wig. Idaho State Police said the suspect was carrying a green backpack.
